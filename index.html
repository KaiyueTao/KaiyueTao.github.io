-------- About Me -------

I'm a senior student at the University of Science and Technology of China(USTC), majoring in Computer Science.

I'm now a member of KDE Lab, working under my advisor Professor Shouhong Wan. My research interests lie in Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence and their real world applications. More Specifically, I'm interested the following topics:

  • Unsupervised/Self-supervised Learning, Meta-learning, Few-shot Learning
  • Multi-modality, including image, video, audio and text, etc.
  • Graph Neural Network

In the summer of 2020 I worked as an computer vision intern at ByteDance. There, I mainly developed an intelligent video-editing pipeline with powerful functions, including raw recording materials clipping, highlight extraction, audio (music) matching, action recognition and video summarization. The pipeline has been widely used in 23+ game trailer generation businesses.

I also participated in the Advanced Medical Imaging (AMI) program in Vascular Imaging Lab at the University of Washington, under the guidance of Prof. Chun Yuan and Dr. Li Chen. I proposed a metric-learing network with episodic training method to learn the feature representation from MR images with extermely-limited labels. The work was summarized into an abstract and submitted to ISMRM2021.

In the summer of 2019 I visited FAiS Lab. I worked on the DNA Hi-C data enhancement with Imgae Super-resolution methods.
